Introduction

Manuscript Fragment Binding

The manuscript fragments used for binding this copy of A Testimonie come from a twelfth-century Bible with commentary by Bede and St. Augustine. The front and back cover both have either part of Acts 4:12 or comments on that verse, suggesting that the two pieces were part of the same folio, though what would have been between them is missing. I will present what I have been able to decipher of the text here, and ask the reader forgive its incompleteness..
